ABOUT US

Pinetree Test Prep Language Centre is a language prep centre located in Coquitlam, BC and provides general counseling on language proficiency examinations, customizable one on one session tutoring, Language Proficiency Examination Preparation classes, and college Preparation exams (SAT / ACT) classes. We also provide simulation tests and self-study area and peer tutoring sessions.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to provide systematic and structured language proficiency courses for students who want to engage and succeed in their academic career.
