Main Features

We have been running “Go to china” youth camp successfully for more than ten years. In ten years, we have brought our students to Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Hong Kong. Our camp provides students with the purest Mandarin learning environment by bringing students to China and facilitating their interaction with native speakers. Students will be living with locals and experience authentic Chinese culture.

Course

Our well-designed course can motivate students’ interest in learning Chinese while improving their listening, speaking, reading and writing, which also enhances their understanding of Chinese art, technology, geography, etc. Studying and having fun in our summer camp in China will be an unforgettable and effective Mandarin learning experience. We provide courses in the morning from 9:00 am to 12:00 pm. Our courses aim to help students to develop their four linguistic skills through interactive in-class teaching and outdoor educational activities. We introduce exciting and interactive teaching methodology into classrooms to ensure every student to masters Mandarin in the most natural and comfortable way.

Sightseeing & Cultural activities

Tours to Chinese Ivy League schools, including Beijing University, Qinghua University, Fudan University, and Shanghai. Tours to top attractions in Beijing, Shanghai, Chongqing and many other cities, such as The Bund in Shanghai, the Great Wall in Beijing, and Ci Qi Kou in Chongiqng. Visits to museums and art galleries.
